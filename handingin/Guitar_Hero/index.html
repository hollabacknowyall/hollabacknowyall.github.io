I shouldn't even have to explain why Guitar Hero is amazing, because you should already know. It was a revolutionary game, allowing those lazy gamers who didn't want to take time to learn guitar play like a pro. I was one of those lazy gamers, and I have to say, nothing made you feel more awesome than absolutely acing a song on hard mode. I mean, even my dad enjoyed playing this game with me. It was truly a family experience.

I would come home and completely destroy a song, and be proud that I was one song closer to defeating each song on the game in every single mode. I never did beat expert mode, but I did beat it in Rockband 3, which sucked btw. Rockband is pretty much trash compared to Guitar Hero.
